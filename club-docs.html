The Developer Student Club is started for students to get technical knowledge like app development, website development and also there are many things which we are going to start in the future. Also, we are focusing on guiding the students and making the real world use project in this club. Our aim is to make this club very valuable in our college campus. We have selected the students in the club by taking the exam’s and also we have recognized the students by meeting them one to one.

The Developer Student Club was started by Ritesh Hon with the guidance of our respected HOD Mr. G. N. Jorvekar sir. In this club we have a core team of 5 members including me.

We all 5 are the core members of the club. Also, we are having the team under us to coordinate with the students. We have taken one boy student as a boy’s coordinator and one girl student as a girl’s coordinator. The coordinators are from FY, SY, TY.


Rules and Regulations

The following block of rules and regulations is focused towards the members of the Developer Student Club. It is expected of the members to abide by them all, or the consequences could lead to the expulsion from the club.

  1. Students are expected to respect the property of others and of the college.
  2. Unauthorised or malicious damage to the property of the college or other persons or entities resulting from organisational activities is prohibited.
  3. Damage done, even by accident, should be reported at once to the appropriate authority. Any damage done shall be made good by the offender.
  4. Club activities that encourage or precipitate disturbances that materially interfere with the regular and orderly operation of the college are prohibited.
  5. The club reserves the right to dismiss any student whose diligence or progress in the club is steadily unsatisfactory or whose conduct is harmful to other students and the club’s image.
  6. Consistent neglect of any subject will not be condoned.
  7. Complete abstinence from violence is expected.

Responsibilities of club member

A dedicated and active club member is essential for the success of the club. A club member must…

  1. Attend meetings and events regularly.
  2. Communicate. Stand up and make your voice heard; reply to surveys, vote.
  3. Be personally involved.
  4. Keep the purpose of the club in mind. If certain goals or methods appear to be outmoded, take the necessary steps to update them.
  5. Preserves amidst shortcomings and difficulties. Expect frustration and difficulty when dealing with people. Be ready to try again and again.
  6. Conserves the club's financial resources. Keep expenses under control.
  7. Prepare for each meeting.
  8. Respect other club members.
